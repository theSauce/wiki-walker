Magra is an unincorporated community in Placer County, California.[1] Magra is located on the former Southern Pacific Railroad (now the Union Pacific Railroad), 4.5 miles (7.2 km) northeast of Colfax.[2] It lies at an elevation of 2887 feet (880 m).[1]
